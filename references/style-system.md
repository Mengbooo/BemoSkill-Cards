# Style System

## Dark

- Black base, white display type, muted gray body text.
- Sparse grid or hairline structure is allowed.
- Accent: teal/green dot or small tag only.
- Keep decoration quiet. Do not use a white filled corner dot.
- Best for engineering, launch notes, technical summaries, and product-system posts.

## Light

- Warm paper base, charcoal display type, muted brown body text.
- Accent: restrained clay/rust dot or label.
- Serif display headings with sans body.
- Avoid heavy grids. Let whitespace carry the composition.
- Best for editorial, strategic, reflective, or calmer technical writing.

## Typography

- Display: `ReactGo Noto Serif SC` from `assets/fonts/noto-serif-sc-900.ttf`.
- Body: Google Sans/Product Sans/Noto Sans SC/system sans stack.
- Keep title text left-aligned.
- Use exact text from the user when supplied.

## Brand Elements

- Logo should appear in the upper-left brand block.
- `ReactGo` or the supplied IP label can appear as a small pill or footer signature.
- Standalone covers should not use sequential page numbers.
